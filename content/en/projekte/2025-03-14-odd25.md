## What are the HackDays?

The **Open Data Day** is an annual international event that highlights the importance of open data worldwide. Local groups organize their own events everywhere — including in Karlsruhe!  
Here, the HackDays: Open Data for Urban Future will take place. Together, we aim to develop innovative solutions for urban challenges using open data.

The Open Data Hack Day is not just for hackers or IT professionals! We follow Wikipedia's definition of "hacking" — it's about finding solutions to challenges using creative methods.  
Everyone can participate — whether beginner or expert, regardless of age or technical experience!

## ![alias="chart" style="width: 32px; display: inline-block !important;  background: none;  shadow: none; position: relative; bottom: -.5rem;  box-shadow: none !important; border: none !important; margin: 0 !important;"](/icons/chartUp.svg) The Data

We have tapped into various data sources:

* Open Data Portals:
    * Karlsruhe/Regional/Baden-Württemberg
        * [Transparency Portal Karlsruhe](https://transparenz.karlsruhe.de/dataset/)
        * [Statistics Portal Karlsruhe](https://web6.karlsruhe.de/Stadtentwicklung/statistik/atlas/)
        * [Re@di Open Data Portal](https://opendata.readi.de/), covering Baden-Baden, Bretten, Bruchsal, Ettlingen, Rastatt, Rheinstetten, Stutensee
        * [Open Data BW](https://www.daten-bw.de/)
        * [Regional Statistics](https://www.regionalstatistik.de/genesis/online/)

    * Nationwide
        * [GovData](https://www.govdata.de/) including ["High Value Datasets"](https://www.govdata.de/suche?tags=hvd)
        * [Destatis](https://www.destatis.de/DE/Home/_inhalt.html)
        * Selection from State Statistics: [Accident Atlas](https://unfallatlas.statistikportal.de)

Besides these dedicated data portals, there are other interesting sources, including administration, (citizen) science, and research. These often come in special formats and may need conversion.

* [Karlsruhe Council Information System](https://sitzungskalender.karlsruhe.de/db/ratsinformation/start)
* 3D Data Karlsruhe - accessible via the Transparency Portal, sometimes requiring conversion. Also relevant for the **Mini-City** project (see Workshops).  
![alias="Zoo3D" style="width: 128px; display: inline-block !important; background: none; shadow: none; position: relative; bottom: -.5rem; box-shadow: none !important; border: none !important; margin: 0 !important;"](/projects/karlsruhe/odd2025/zoo3d.png)
* *IDEAL Household Energy Dataset* from FZI, containing energy use data from 255 households in the UK.
* [Next-Bike Station Data](https://api.nextbike.net/maps/nextbike-live.json?countries=de) — historical data set available, as online data is only temporarily accessible.
* [Autonomous Driving Data](https://github.com/fzi-forschungszentrum-informatik/test-area-autonomous-driving-dataset): Mobility data, trajectory data at a research intersection – the TAF-BW dataset.  
![alias="TAF" style="width: 128px; display: inline-block !important; background: none; shadow: none; position: relative; bottom: -.5rem; box-shadow: none !important; border: none !important; margin: 0 !important;"](/projects/karlsruhe/odd2025/taf.gif)
* [City Cycling 2023/2024](https://www.stadtradeln.de/home) – routes data from Karlsruhe cyclists.  
![alias="Radeln" style="width: 128px; display: inline-block !important; background: none; shadow: none; position: relative; bottom: -.5rem; box-shadow: none !important; border: none !important; margin: 0 !important;"](/projects/karlsruhe/odd2025/radeln.png)
* ZKM Visitor Movement Data: special *mobility data* from the *Renaissance 3.0* exhibition at ZKM — great for visualizations.  
![alias="ZKM" style="width: 128px; display: inline-block !important; background: none; shadow: none; position: relative; bottom: -.5rem; box-shadow: none !important; border: none !important; margin: 0 !important;"](/projects/karlsruhe/odd2025/zkm-tracks-small.jpg)
* Karlsruhe's Climate Protection Concept offers data on planned measures. There's a [prototype dashboard](https://dashboard.daten.city/) for visualizing this and other datasets.

**All datasets (and metadata where available) have been downloaded. The data is on a shared drive and can be copied to your laptops.** [Data abstracts with links](/projects/karlsruhe/odd2025/daten.pdf)
